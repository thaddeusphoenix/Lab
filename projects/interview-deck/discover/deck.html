What I'd Do Differently
Track everything from day one
Marketing, sales, implementation, adoption, growth — qualitative and quantitative, always. If it's not tracked, it's not managed.
GTM is a product requirement
Validate every phase of the process with each prototype. The product team needs to live the customer's Time-to-Value journey before handing it to anyone else.
Enterprise adoption is slow — own it
Your customer's problem is your problem. If their adoption timeline blocks your ability to move fast, that's a product problem — not an implementation one.
The Through Line

Foundation first.
Then scale.

When the crisis hit, I treated it as a product problem — not someone else's. That's what turned fifteen potential failures into three real customers.
